/README.md:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1 | This is a modified fork of shadcn_flutter and includes resources & merged code from Phosphor Icons & Ionicons. It is designed for quickly creating apps. 
 2 | 
 3 | #### [Documentation](https://tome.arcane.art) | [Pub.dev](https://pub.dev/packages/arcane) | [GitHub](https://github.com/ArcaneArts/arcane)
 4 | 
 5 | ## Forked / Modified Packages
 6 | - [shadcn_flutter](https://pub.dev/packages/shadcn_flutter) for general widgets & overall UI. 
 7 | - [phosphor_flutter](https://pub.dev/packages/phosphor_flutter) & [ionicons](https://pub.dev/packages/ionicons) for icons
 8 | - [blurme](https://pub.dev/packages/blurme) for blur effects (needed to modify) license unneeded as it says "Use however you want".
 9 | 
10 | ## Initial Setup
11 | 
12 | Please read the [Installation](https://tome.arcane.art/#/installation) guide for more information.
13 | 
14 | # Addon Packages
15 | - [arcane_fluf](https://pub.dev/packages/arcane_crud) for using flutterfire stack (which utilizes: )
16 |   - [arcane_auth](https://pub.dev/packages/arcane_auth) for firebase authentication
17 |   - [arcane_user](https://pub.dev/packages/arcane_user) for user data models in firestore
18 |   - [arcane_fire](https://pub.dev/packages/arcane_fire) for initializing firebase with arcane
19 | 
20 | ```dart
21 | void main() => runApp("mtg_tesseract", ArcaneWindow(
22 |   windowColor: Colors.transparent,
23 |   child: ArcaneFluf(
24 |           options: DefaultFirebaseOptions.currentPlatform,
25 |           onRegisterCrud: $registerCrud,
26 |           onRegisterUserService: () => services().register<UserService>(() => UserService()),
27 |     child: MyCustomInitializer(
28 |       ///////////
29 |       child: const TesseractApp()
30 |     )
31 |   )
32 | ));
33 | ```

/ROADMAP.md:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 | # Roadmap
2 | 
3 | * [x] Imperative Routing with Widgets instead of string named routes, but with the benefits of named routes such as path variables, query parameters and url generation.
